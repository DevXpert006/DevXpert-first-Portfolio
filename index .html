Hi, I’m Hussein Taiwo, a passionate and creative Frontend Developer based in Nigeria, with a focus on building modern, responsive websites and digital solutions that solve real problems and inspire users.

With strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript I’ve built a variety of projects, including a full Hospital Management System with user authentication, dashboards, email verification, profile editing, real-time Firebase database integration, and dynamic appointment booking features.

I’ve also developed:
